Serving your community with healthy, nutritious, and delicious food is a responsibility that we at HomeCooked, and you, as a food provider, take seriously. We, and you, recognize the importance of and hereby abide by the following beliefs:

  • The food you provide contributes to the health and well being of your community.
  • Your ability and certification qualify you to serve the public by applying safe food practices, nutritional acumen, healthy and quality food service techniques to everything you prepare and serve.
  • You are individually responsible for your actions and the quality of services that you provide.
  • You pledge to conduct yourself with honesty, integrity and fairness and strive to provide all services competently.
  • You will always use only the freshest meat, fish, vegetables and fruits available and quality ingredients.
  • You strive to comply with all applicable laws and regulations concerning food preparation including those that promote public health and safety.
  • You will provide full and accurate information regarding ingredients and the preparation of meals.
  • You will never compromise on quality and safety, at any cost.
  • ALWAYS COOK AS IF IT WILL BE THE LAST DISH YOU ARE EVER MAKE!
